FAQ

How to register?

To register, click on the "Sign in" button located at the top right corner of the homepage. Follow the prompts to create a new account by providing the required information.

How to add movies to my watchlist?

To add movies to your watchlist, simply click on the "Watchlist" button next to the movie you want to save. You can access your watchlist anytime by clicking on the "Watchlist" button in the header.

How to search for a specific movie or TV show?

Use the search bar located at the top center of the homepage. Type in the name of the movie or TV show you are looking for and click the "Search" button to see the results.
